The Boyd Family is originally from St. Johnston's Donegal, Ireland.


Our family members are Irish, Italian, English, German, Dutch, and Vietnamese descent.

My grandfather Richard Boyd Sr. Born December 20th, 1950, passed away April 27th, 2019. He was a Philadelphia fire fighter for 32 years.

My father Richard Boyd Jr. Born February 27th, 1974, passed away October 31, 2019. He was a restaurant cook.

I have never met my great-grand parents on my dad's side of the family whom were Wilbur Boyd, and Naomi Vaughn.

I have been told Naomi Vaughn was a minister.

My great grand parents on my mom's side were named Michael and Winnie, I have met Winnie when I was really young, but not Michael.

My middle name is Vaughn which was Naomi's maiden name.

My wife's name is Hang Thuy Nguyen-Boyd, which is where the Vietnamese descent comes from.

I met my wife when I was stationed in Yokosuka, Japan as a Sonar Technician. Her home town in Japan is Hiratsuka.

The places I have been in the world are: Japan, South Korea, India, Guam, Saipan, Tonga, Australia, America Samoa, Singapore, Okinawa, and Hong Kong.

I am building this website in dedication to the entire Boyd Family and future generations.
